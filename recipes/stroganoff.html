Ground Beef Stroganoff Noodles

Ground Beef Stroganoff Noodles is a delicious and comforting dish made with tender ground beef, savory mushrooms, and creamy sauce. It is served over a bed of noodles, creating a hearty and satisfying meal. The combination of flavors and textures makes this dish a family favorite that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup thinly sliced mushrooms
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 ⅓ tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons vodka
  • 2 ½ cups beef broth, or as needed
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 ½ cups egg noodles
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons fresh sliced chives or green onions

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter and vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and salt. Cook until the mushrooms are tender and lightly browned.
  3. Add the diced onion and ground beef. Cook until the beef is browned and cooked through.
  4. Season with black pepper and cayenne pepper.
  5. Add the minced garlic and flour. Stir well to combine.
  6. Add the vodka and beef broth. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  7. Add the water and egg noodles. Cook until the noodles are tender, stirring occasionally.
  8. Remove from heat and stir in the sour cream.
  9. Garnish with fresh sliced chives or green onions.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
