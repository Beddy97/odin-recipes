Super Nachos

Super Nachos are a delicious and satisfying dish that is perfect for any occasion. They are made with tortilla chips, topped with seasoned ground beef, melted cheese, and a variety of toppings such as jalapenos, sour cream, and guacamole. Super Nachos are a crowd-pleasing appetizer or snack that is sure to be a hit at your next gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 bag tortilla chips
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup guacamole
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro

How To Make Them

  • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  • Add the taco seasoning and water according to the package instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  • Arrange the tortilla chips on a large baking sheet.
  • Sprinkle the seasoned ground beef over the tortilla chips.
  • Top with the shredded cheddar cheese.
  • Bake for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Remove from the oven and top with the diced tomatoes, sliced black olives, sliced jalapenos, sour cream, guacamole, and chopped cilantro.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!
